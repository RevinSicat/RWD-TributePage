Jose Rizal

He is considered a national hero (pambansang bayani) of the Philippines.

José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda was a Filipino nationalist, writer and polymath active at the end of the Spanish colonial period of the Philippines.

  • Full Name: José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonzo Realonda
  • Born: June 19, 1861 – Calamba, Laguna, Philippines
  • Died: December 30, 1896 – Executed by firing squad in Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park), Manila
  • Profession: Ophthalmologist, writer, poet, and nationalist
  • Known For: Inspiring the Philippine Revolution through his writings
  • Notable Works: Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo – novels exposing Spanish colonial abuse
  • Advocacy: Peaceful reform, education, and freedom from Spanish rule
  • Legacy: His martyrdom ignited Filipino nationalism, leading to independence
